What Determines Bitcoin Rates And Prices?

  • Bitcoin is determined by the market forces of demand and supply.

  • When the demand for Bitcoin increases in the market, the price rises and vice versa.
    On the contrary, when the supply of Bitcoin decreases, the price rises since the shortage created causes consumers to willingly pay a higher price for it.
